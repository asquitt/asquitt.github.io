Understanding Invisalign Costs in Washington, DC

When searching for "Invisalign cost near me" in Washington, DC, you'll find that pricing varies significantly based on treatment complexity and provider expertise. At Connecticut Ave Dental Studio, we believe in transparent pricing and affordable solutions for every patient.

What Affects Your Invisalign Cost?

Several key factors influence the total cost of your Invisalign treatment in 2026. The complexity of your case determines the number of aligners needed and treatment duration.

  • Severity of misalignment and bite issues
  • Treatment duration (typically 12-24 months)
  • Number of aligner sets required
  • Additional procedures like attachments or refinements
  • Geographic location and provider expertise

Invisalign Cost Breakdown for DC Residents

In Washington, DC, Invisalign treatment typically ranges from $3,500 to $8,000. Our practice offers competitive pricing with flexible payment options to make treatment accessible.

  • Invisalign Express (minor corrections): $3,500 - $4,500
  • Invisalign Lite (moderate cases): $4,500 - $6,000
  • Invisalign Full (comprehensive treatment): $6,000 - $8,000
  • Invisalign Teen (adolescent treatment): $5,000 - $7,500
