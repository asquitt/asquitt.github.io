Invisalign treatment costs vary based on your unique needs. According to the American Association of Orthodontists, most patients invest between $3,000 and $7,000 for their complete treatment. Several factors affect your final cost:

  • Treatment duration needed

Our team provides detailed cost estimates during your consultation. We explain exactly what's included in your treatment plan and help you understand all available financing options.
