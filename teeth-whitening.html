Maintaining Your Brighter Smile Long-Term

After your professional teeth whitening treatment, simple care steps help maintain your brilliant results for years:

  • Brush twice daily with whitening toothpaste recommended by our team
  • Limit staining foods and drinks like coffee, red wine, and dark berries
  • Use a straw when drinking dark beverages to minimize contact
  • Schedule regular dental cleanings and checkups every six months
  • Avoid tobacco products which cause rapid re-staining

Following these guidelines can extend your whitening results for 2-3 years, protecting your investment in your beautiful smile.
