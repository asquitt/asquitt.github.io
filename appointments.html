Professional Dental services tailored to your needs

At Connecticut Ave Dental Studio, we make dental appointment booking simple and stress-free for Washington DC families. General Dentistry

Our foundation of care includes routine checkups, fillings, and preventive treatments to maintain your oral health. We focus on early detection and gentle treatment approaches, ensuring your family receives comfortable, thorough care. Regular visits help prevent serious dental issues and keep your smile healthy.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Transform your smile with our aesthetic dental treatments, including teeth whitening, veneers, and smile makeovers. We understand how important confidence is, and our cosmetic services are tailored to enhance your natural beauty. Let us help you achieve the radiant smile you've always dreamed of having.

Emergency Dental Care

When dental emergencies strike, we're here to provide immediate relief and expert care. Our team understands that dental pain can't wait, which is why we offer prompt emergency appointments. From severe toothaches to dental trauma, we'll get you comfortable and back to your normal routine quickly.

Dental Implants

Replace missing teeth with our state-of-the-art dental implant solutions that look and feel completely natural. Our implant process restores both function and confidence, allowing you to eat, speak, and smile without worry. Experience the permanent solution that gives you back your complete, beautiful smile.

Professional Cleanings

Our thorough dental cleanings go beyond what you can achieve at home, removing plaque and tartar buildup. Our gentle hygienists provide comfortable cleanings that leave your teeth feeling fresh and smooth. Regular professional cleanings are essential for preventing gum disease and maintaining optimal oral health.
