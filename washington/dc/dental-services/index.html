Frequently Asked Questions About Dental Services in Washington

What dental services do you offer?

Connecticut Ave Dental Studio provides comprehensive dental care including routine cleanings, fillings, crowns, root canals, extractions, and cosmetic treatments. We focus on preventive care and restorative procedures to maintain optimal oral health.

How often should I visit the dentist?

Most patients benefit from dental checkups and cleanings every six months. However, your specific needs may vary based on your oral health condition and risk factors.

Do you accept dental insurance?

We work with most major dental insurance plans. Contact us to verify your specific coverage and benefits before your appointment.

What should I expect during my first visit?

Your initial appointment includes a comprehensive examination, digital X-rays if needed, and a thorough cleaning. We'll discuss your dental history and create a personalized treatment plan.

How do I prepare for my dental appointment?

Bring your insurance information, list current medications, and arrive a few minutes early. Continue your normal oral hygiene routine before your visit.

Do you offer emergency dental services?

Yes, we provide emergency dental care for urgent situations like severe pain, broken teeth, or dental trauma.
