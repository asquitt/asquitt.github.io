Meet Our Dental Practice Staff - Connecticut Ave Dental Studio

Professional Dental services tailored to your needs

At Connecticut Ave Dental Studio, our experienced dental practice staff is dedicated to providing comprehensive, compassionate care for every member of your family. We combine advanced dental techniques with a gentle approach to ensure your comfort and optimal oral health. Our team is committed to building lasting relationships with Cleveland Park families and Washington DC residents.

General Dentistry

Our skilled dental practice staff provides complete preventive and restorative care to maintain your family's oral health. From routine checkups and fillings to root canals and crowns, we offer comprehensive treatment in a comfortable, welcoming environment that puts patients at ease.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Transform your smile with our cosmetic dental services, expertly delivered by our experienced team. We offer teeth whitening, veneers, and smile makeovers designed to boost your confidence. Our dental practice staff works closely with you to achieve the beautiful, natural-looking results you desire.

Emergency Dental Care

When dental emergencies strike, our compassionate staff is here to provide prompt, effective relief. We understand that dental pain and accidents can't wait, which is why we prioritize urgent cases and offer same-day appointments whenever possible to restore your comfort quickly.

Dental Implants

Our dental practice staff specializes in dental implant placement and restoration, offering a permanent solution for missing teeth. Using state-of-the-art technology and proven techniques, we help restore your smile's function and appearance while preserving your jawbone health for years to come.

Professional Cleanings

Regular professional cleanings by our gentle hygienists are essential for preventing gum disease and maintaining optimal oral health. Our thorough cleaning process removes plaque and tartar buildup while our staff provides personalized advice to help you maintain your smile between visits.
