Orthodontic Treatment Options: Braces & Clear Aligners in Washington DC

Professional Dental services tailored to your needs

At Connecticut Ave Dental Studio, we understand that achieving a beautiful, healthy smile is deeply personal. Our comprehensive orthodontic treatment options are designed to meet your unique needs while ensuring your comfort throughout the journey. We're here to help you discover the perfect solution for your smile transformation.

Traditional Metal Braces

Traditional metal braces remain one of the most effective orthodontic solutions for complex alignment issues. Using high-quality brackets and wires, we gradually guide your teeth into their ideal positions. Modern braces are more comfortable than ever, offering reliable results for patients of all ages.

Clear Ceramic Braces

For patients who prefer a more discreet orthodontic option, ceramic braces blend seamlessly with your natural tooth color. These tooth-colored brackets provide the same effective treatment as traditional braces while maintaining a more subtle appearance. Perfect for adults and teens who want effective results.

Invisalign Clear Aligners

Experience the freedom of nearly invisible orthodontic treatment with Invisalign clear aligners. These custom-made, removable trays gradually straighten your teeth without metal brackets or wires. Enjoy the convenience of eating your favorite foods and maintaining excellent oral hygiene while achieving your perfect smile.

Invisalign Teen

Specially designed for teenage lifestyles, Invisalign Teen offers all the benefits of clear aligner therapy with added features for growing smiles. Blue compliance indicators help track wearing time, while replacement aligners account for active teen schedules. Your teenager can maintain confidence throughout treatment.

Adult Orthodontic Treatment

It's never too late to achieve the smile you deserve. Our adult orthodontic treatments are tailored to fit busy professional lifestyles and address unique concerns like gum health and existing dental work. Whether you choose braces or clear aligners, we'll create a comfortable treatment plan that respects your time.

Orthodontic Consultations

Begin your smile journey with a comprehensive orthodontic consultation at our Cleveland Park location. We'll evaluate your unique needs, discuss all available treatment options, and create a personalized plan that fits your lifestyle and budget. Our compassionate team ensures you feel informed and confident.
