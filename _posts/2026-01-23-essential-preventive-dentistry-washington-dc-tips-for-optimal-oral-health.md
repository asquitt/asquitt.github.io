# Essential Preventive Dental Care Tips for Optimal Oral Health

Poor oral health affects 90% of Washington DC adults, yet most dental problems are entirely preventable. At Connecticut Ave Dental Studio, serving the Washington DC community, we believe smart prevention beats expensive treatment every time.

## Daily Habits That Protect Your Smile

Master these fundamentals for effective DC preventive dental care:

- Brush twice daily with fluoride toothpaste for two minutes
- Floss daily to remove plaque between teeth
- Use antimicrobial mouthwash to reduce bacteria
- Replace your toothbrush every three months
- Limit sugary and acidic foods that damage enamel
