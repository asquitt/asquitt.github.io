## What Are Dental Implants?

Dental implants are titanium posts surgically placed into your jawbone. They function as artificial tooth roots, supporting crowns, bridges, or dentures. The process typically takes several months as the implant fuses with your bone.

## What to Expect During Treatment

Your implant journey begins with thorough evaluation. Your dentist will assess bone density, discuss goals, and create a personalized treatment plan.

The procedure uses local anesthesia. Most patients experience minimal discomfort and return to normal activities within days.

Healing takes 3-6 months as the implant integrates with your jawbone. Once complete, you'll receive your custom crown for natural-looking results.
