# Chinese Restaurant Recommendations DC: A Foodie's Complete Guide to Szechuan, Cantonese, and Regional Chinese Dining

Washington DC's Chinese restaurant scene rivals any major city, offering authentic flavors from across China's diverse regions. These Chinese restaurant recommendations DC food lovers swear by showcase everything from fiery Szechuan dishes to delicate Cantonese dim sum.

## Top Szechuan Restaurants

**China Garden** (2801 New Mexico Ave NW) delivers authentic Szechuan heat with signature mapo tofu and dan dan noodles. Complex spice blends and mouth-tingling peppercorns create the numbing sensation true Szechuan cuisine demands.

**Szechuan Pavilion** (1820 K St NW) excels in kung pao chicken and twice-cooked pork, perfect for downtown diners seeking genuine regional flavors.

## Cantonese Dining Excellence

**Full Kee** (509 H St NW) in Chinatown serves exceptional dim sum with perfectly steamed har gow and crispy char siu bao. Weekend dim sum service showcases traditional Cantonese techniques emphasizing natural flavors and precise wok hei cooking.

## Regional Specialties Worth Exploring

- **Hunan cuisine**: Intense heat without numbing spices at Dupont Circle establishments
- **Shanghai dishes**: Sweet and savory combinations in Adams Morgan
- **Beijing specialties**: Authentic Peking duck in Rockville locations
- **Taiwanese offerings**: Night market favorites citywide
