# 10 Best Cosmetic Dentistry Options in DC: Transform Your Smile Today

Finding the right cosmetic dentistry options in DC can transform your smile and boost your confidence. Washington DC offers world-class dental care with modern technology and patient-first approaches. The city's competitive dental market attracts top specialists who train at prestigious institutions like Georgetown University School of Dentistry.

DC's unique position as the nation's capital creates a demanding professional environment where appearance matters. This drives innovation in cosmetic dentistry, with practices investing in the latest technology to serve diplomats, politicians, and business leaders.

## Top 10 Cosmetic Dentistry Procedures

### 1. Teeth Whitening Washington DC ($300-$800)
Professional teeth whitening removes years of stains and discoloration. Results appear in just one visit, brightening your smile up to eight shades.

### 2. Dental Veneers DC ($800-$2,500 per tooth)
Porcelain veneers cover chips, gaps, and discoloration. These thin shells create a perfect smile that lasts 10-15 years with proper care.

### 3. Invisalign Clear Aligners ($3,000-$8,000)
Straighten teeth without metal braces. Clear aligners are removable and nearly invisible during treatment.

### 4. Dental Bonding ($100-$400 per tooth)
Composite resin repairs minor chips and gaps quickly. This affordable option completes in one appointment.

### 5. Smile Makeovers ($5,000-$30,000)
Combine multiple treatments for complete smile transformation. Custom plans address your specific needs and goals.

### 6. Gum Contouring ($300-$3,000)
Reshape uneven gum lines for better smile proportions. Laser technology ensures precise, comfortable treatment.

### 7. Dental Crowns ($800-$1,500 per tooth)
Restore damaged teeth with natural-looking crowns. Modern materials match your existing tooth color perfectly.

### 8. Dental Implants ($3,000-$5,000 per tooth)
Replace missing teeth with permanent solutions. Implants look and function like natural teeth, providing a lifetime solution for tooth loss.

### 9. Inlays and Onlays ($650-$1,200 per tooth)
Custom restorations repair larger cavities while preserving tooth structure. They offer superior durability compared to fillings.

### 10. Full Mouth Reconstruction ($20,000-$80,000)
Comprehensive treatment addresses multiple dental issues simultaneously. This extensive process rebuilds your entire smile using combinations of crowns, veneers, implants, and orthodontics.
