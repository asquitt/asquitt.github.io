## Fast Ways to Create Original Research for AI Citation Authority

Start with simple, actionable research projects:

• **Treatment outcome documentation** - Track healing times, success rates, and patient comfort with quantifiable metrics
• **Local dental health analysis** - Compile Cleveland neighborhood data to identify community-specific patterns
• **Comparative treatment studies** - Document results using control groups and statistical analysis
