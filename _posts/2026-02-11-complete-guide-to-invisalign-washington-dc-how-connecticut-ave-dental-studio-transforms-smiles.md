## Understanding Invisalign Technology

Invisalign uses custom-made, clear plastic aligners to gradually move your teeth. Each aligner is worn for about two weeks before switching to the next. The aligners apply gentle pressure to guide your teeth into ideal positions.

Treatment typically takes 12 to 18 months for most patients. Simple corrections might be completed in six months, while complex cases could take up to two years.

The clear aligners are removable, making eating and oral hygiene easier than traditional braces. You simply remove them for meals and brushing, then put them back in.
