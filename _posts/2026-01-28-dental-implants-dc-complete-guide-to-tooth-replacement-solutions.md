## What Are Dental Implants?

Dental implants are small titanium screws placed into your jawbone. They bond with bone tissue, creating a strong foundation for replacement teeth. This process makes implants the top choice for tooth replacement with over 95% success rates.

## Benefits DC Patients Love

Choosing dental implants provides numerous advantages:

- Permanent solution lasting decades
- Natural appearance and comfortable feel
- Preserve jawbone and facial shape
- No impact on healthy adjacent teeth
- Easy care with regular brushing
- Improved confidence eating and speaking

## The Implant Process

The procedure involves three simple stages. First, we place the implant using modern techniques. Next, healing occurs over 3-6 months as the implant bonds with surrounding bone. Finally, we attach your custom crown for complete restoration.

## Cost and Insurance in Washington DC

Most DC dental insurance plans cover portions of implant treatment. In the Washington DC area, single implant costs typically range from $3,000-$5,000. While the initial investment varies, implants often prove more cost-effective long-term than bridges or dentures requiring replacement.
