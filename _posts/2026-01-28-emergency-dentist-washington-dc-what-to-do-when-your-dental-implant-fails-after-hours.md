## Signs Your Dental Implant Needs Emergency Care

Call an emergency dentist in Washington DC if you notice:

- Severe pain or throbbing
- Loose or moving implant
- Heavy bleeding around the site
- Pus or discharge
- Facial swelling
- Fever with dental pain

## What to Do Before Getting Emergency Care

While seeking urgent dental care in DC:

- Rinse gently with warm salt water
- Use a cold compress for swelling
- Take pain medication as directed
