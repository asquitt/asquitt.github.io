## When Kids Need Root Canal Treatment

Children require root canal treatment when infection reaches the tooth's nerve. Common causes include:

- Deep cavities from poor oral hygiene
- Tooth trauma from sports or accidents
- Cracked teeth exposing the pulp
- Severe tooth pain or swelling

Early treatment saves natural teeth and prevents complications. The procedure removes infected pulp while preserving tooth structure for healthy development.
