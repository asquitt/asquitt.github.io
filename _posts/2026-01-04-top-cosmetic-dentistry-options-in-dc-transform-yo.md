## Popular Cosmetic Dental Treatments in Washington DC

### Professional Teeth Whitening

Professional teeth whitening remains one of the most requested cosmetic procedures. A qualified **cosmetic dentist Washington DC** can deliver results that are:

- Up to 8 shades whiter in a single session
- Safe for sensitive teeth
- Long-lasting with proper care
- Customized to your desired shade

Many practices in Dupont Circle and Georgetown offer both in-office ($300-$800) and take-home whitening options ($200-$400).

### Porcelain Veneers

Dental veneers specialists craft ultra-thin porcelain shells that cover the front surface of your teeth. Veneers address multiple concerns simultaneously:

- Discolored or stained teeth
- Chipped or cracked enamel
- Gaps between teeth
- Slightly crooked teeth
- Worn-down teeth

The process requires two visits and can last 10-15 years. Expect to invest $1,200-$2,500 per tooth for premium porcelain veneers.

### Dental Bonding

For minor imperfections, dental bonding offers a cost-effective solution ($150-$400 per tooth). This procedure uses tooth-colored resin to:

- Repair small chips or cracks
- Close minor gaps
- Reshape uneven teeth
- Cover surface stains

Bonding can usually be completed in one appointment and costs significantly less than veneers.

## Advanced Cosmetic Procedures

### Smile Makeovers

A comprehensive smile makeover combines multiple treatments for dramatic results. Your **cosmetic dentist Washington DC** creates a customized treatment plan that may include teeth whitening, veneers, orthodontic treatment, gum contouring, and dental crowns or implants.

This holistic approach ensures all elements work together harmoniously. Complete makeovers typically range from $5,000-$25,000 depending on complexity.

### Invisalign Clear Aligners

For adults wanting straighter teeth without traditional braces, Invisalign offers a discreet solution ($3,000-$7,000). These clear, removable aligners gradually shift teeth into proper alignment over 12-18 months.

Benefits include:
- Nearly invisible appearance
- Removable for eating and cleaning
- No dietary restrictions
- Comfortable fit
