# Best Foods for Dental Health: Nutrition Tips from Connecticut Ave Dental Studio

## Calcium-Rich Champions

Dairy products rebuild tooth enamel through calcium and phosphorus:

- Milk
- Cheese (neutralizes mouth acid)
- Greek yogurt
- Leafy greens like kale

These minerals remineralize weakened enamel, reversing early decay.

## Crunchy Fruits and Vegetables

Crispy produce mechanically cleans teeth while stimulating saliva production:

- Apples
- Carrots
- Celery
- Bell peppers

Increased saliva flow naturally rinses away food particles and neutralizes harmful acids.

## Protein Powerhouses

Lean proteins supply phosphorus, which partners with calcium for enamel protection:

- Fish (especially salmon with vitamin D)
- Eggs
- Chicken
- Nuts and seeds

Vitamin D enhances calcium absorption, maximizing enamel strengthening.

## Smart Hydration

Water flushes bacteria and food debris while maintaining optimal saliva levels. Choose water over sugary drinks that feed cavity-causing bacteria.

## Foods to Limit

Minimize sugary snacks, sodas, and sticky candies. These create acidic conditions that erode enamel and promote bacterial growth.
