About Sunrise Dental Care

Sunrise Dental Care was founded in 2008 by Dr. Maria Gonzalez with a simple mission: to provide exceptional dental care in a warm, family-friendly environment. What started as a small practice with two treatment rooms has grown into one of Austin's most trusted dental offices, serving thousands of patients across the greater Austin metro area.

Our practice is built on the belief that dental health is an essential part of overall well-being. We take a holistic approach to dentistry, considering each patient's complete health history, lifestyle, and goals when developing treatment plans. This personalized approach has earned us a loyal patient base and numerous referrals from satisfied families.

Meet Our Team

Dr. Maria Gonzalez, DDS

Dr. Gonzalez graduated from the University of Texas Health Science Center at San Antonio School of Dentistry in 2005. She completed her residency at Dell Seton Medical Center and has been practicing in Austin ever since. She is a member of the American Dental Association, Texas Dental Association, and the Academy of General Dentistry. Her areas of expertise include cosmetic dentistry, implant placement, and pediatric care.

Dr. James Chen, DMD

Dr. Chen joined Sunrise Dental in 2015 after completing his Doctor of Dental Medicine degree at Tufts University School of Dental Medicine. He specializes in endodontics and restorative dentistry, bringing over a decade of experience in root canal therapy and complex crown work. Patients appreciate his calm demeanor and meticulous attention to detail during procedures.

Dr. Aisha Patel, DDS

The newest addition to our team, Dr. Patel brings a fresh perspective and expertise in digital dentistry and minimally invasive techniques. She earned her DDS from the University of Michigan School of Dentistry and completed advanced training in orthodontics. She is passionate about using technology to improve patient outcomes and reduce treatment times.

Our Facility

Our modern, 5,000-square-foot facility on Medical Parkway features eight fully equipped treatment rooms, a dedicated pediatric suite, and a state-of-the-art sterilization center. We invested in the latest dental technology to provide our patients with the most accurate diagnoses and effective treatments available.

Our technology includes cone beam CT scanning for three-dimensional imaging, digital impressions using iTero scanners that eliminate messy traditional molds, CEREC same-day crown fabrication, and soft tissue laser technology for minimally invasive gum treatments. We also offer nitrous oxide sedation and oral sedation options for patients who experience dental anxiety.

The office was designed with patient comfort in mind. Our waiting area features complimentary beverages, free Wi-Fi, and a children's play corner. Each treatment room has a ceiling-mounted television so patients can watch their favorite shows during procedures. We strive to make every visit as pleasant and stress-free as possible.

Our Values

  • Compassion: We treat every patient with kindness, empathy, and respect, understanding that dental visits can be anxiety-inducing for many people.
  • Excellence: We maintain the highest standards of clinical care through ongoing education, advanced technology, and evidence-based treatment protocols.
  • Integrity: We provide honest recommendations and transparent pricing, never suggesting unnecessary procedures.
  • Community: We are proud to give back to Austin through free dental clinics, school education programs, and sponsorship of local youth sports teams.
