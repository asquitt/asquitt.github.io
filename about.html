About Connecticut Ave Dental Studio

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Connecticut Ave Dental Studio has been providing exceptional dental care to the Washington DC community for over 15 years. Located in the heart of Cleveland Park on Connecticut Avenue NW, we combine cutting-edge dental technology with personalized patient care.

Our practice serves patients from throughout the DC metro area, including Cleveland Park, Woodley Park, Tenleytown, Chevy Chase, and Bethesda. We believe that everyone deserves access to quality dental care in a comfortable, welcoming environment.
