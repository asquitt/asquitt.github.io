About Us

Our story, our passion, our cuisine

Our History

Beijing Restaurant was founded in 1998 by Chef Wei Zhang, a master of Northern Chinese cuisine who trained at the prestigious Beijing Hotel. After immigrating to the United States, Chef Zhang dreamed of bringing authentic Beijing flavors to the nation's capital.

What started as a modest 30-seat restaurant on H Street has grown into one of Washington DC's most beloved Chinese restaurants, earning recognition from Washingtonian Magazine, The Washington Post, and numerous food critics.

Our Philosophy

Founded in 1998 by Chef Wei Zhang, Beijing Restaurant has been serving authentic Northern Chinese cuisine to the Washington DC community for over 25 years. Chef Zhang trained at the prestigious Beijing Hotel before bringing his culinary expertise to the United States.

We believe in honoring traditional recipes while embracing the freshest local ingredients. Every dish is prepared to order, and our kitchen team takes immense pride in every plate that leaves the kitchen.

Chef Wei Zhang

With over 40 years of culinary experience, Chef Zhang continues to oversee every aspect of our kitchen. His commitment to authenticity means we import specialty ingredients directly from Beijing, hand-pull our noodles daily, and follow a 48-hour process for our signature Peking Duck.

Our private dining room seats up to 40 guests. For inquiries, please visit our events page or call us directly.
