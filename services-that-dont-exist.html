Professional Dental services tailored to your needs

At Connecticut Ave Dental Studio, we're committed to providing comprehensive dental care that keeps your smile healthy and bright. Our experienced team offers a full range of services designed to meet all your oral health needs in a comfortable, welcoming environment.

General Dentistry

Our comprehensive general dentistry services include routine cleanings, exams, and preventive care to maintain your oral health. We focus on early detection and treatment of dental issues, helping you avoid more complex procedures while keeping your teeth and gums in optimal condition.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Transform your smile with our cosmetic dentistry options, including teeth whitening, veneers, and smile makeovers. We combine artistry with advanced techniques to enhance your natural beauty and boost your confidence, creating the radiant smile you've always wanted.

Restorative Dentistry

When dental problems occur, our restorative services can repair and rebuild your smile. From fillings and crowns to bridges and implants, we use high-quality materials and proven techniques to restore both function and aesthetics to your teeth.

Periodontal Care

Maintain healthy gums with our specialized periodontal treatments. We provide deep cleanings, scaling and root planing, and ongoing maintenance to prevent and treat gum disease, protecting your overall oral health and preventing tooth loss.

Emergency Dental Care

Dental emergencies can happen anytime, and we're here to help when you need us most. Our prompt emergency care addresses urgent issues like severe pain, broken teeth, or dental trauma, providing immediate relief and treatment.

Oral Surgery

Our skilled team performs various oral surgery procedures, including tooth extractions, wisdom tooth removal, and preparatory work for dental implants. We prioritize your comfort and safety throughout every surgical procedure, ensuring optimal healing and results.
