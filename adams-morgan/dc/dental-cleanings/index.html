Frequently Asked Questions About dental cleanings in Adams Morgan

How often should I get dental cleanings?

Most dental professionals recommend cleanings every six months to maintain optimal oral health and prevent plaque buildup.

What happens during a dental cleaning appointment?

Professional dental cleanings typically include plaque and tartar removal, tooth polishing, and a thorough examination of your teeth and gums.

Are dental cleanings covered by insurance?

Many dental insurance plans cover routine cleanings as preventive care, though coverage varies by plan.

How long does a dental cleaning take?

Most cleaning appointments last between 45 minutes to an hour, depending on your individual needs.

Do dental cleanings hurt?

Professional cleanings are generally comfortable, though some sensitivity may occur if you have gum inflammation or tartar buildup.
