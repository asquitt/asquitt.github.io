Transform Your Smile with Cosmetic Dental Procedures in Washington DC

Professional Dental services tailored to your needs

At Connecticut Avenue Dental, we believe everyone deserves a smile they can feel confident about. Our comprehensive cosmetic dental procedures are designed to enhance both the beauty and health of your smile, using the latest techniques and technology. We're committed to helping Washington DC families achieve their dream smiles in a comfortable, caring environment.

Porcelain Veneers

Transform your smile with custom-crafted porcelain veneers that correct chips, stains, gaps, and minor misalignments. These ultra-thin shells are bonded to your natural teeth, creating a flawless, natural-looking smile that can last for years with proper care.

Professional Teeth Whitening

Brighten your smile up to eight shades with our safe, effective whitening treatments. Whether you choose our in-office procedure for immediate results or take-home kits for gradual whitening, we'll help you achieve the radiant smile you've always wanted.

Dental Bonding

Repair minor imperfections like chips, cracks, or gaps with tooth-colored composite resin bonding. This affordable, minimally invasive procedure can be completed in a single visit, instantly improving your smile's appearance while preserving your natural tooth structure.

Invisalign Clear Aligners

Straighten your teeth discreetly with Invisalign's virtually invisible aligners. Perfect for busy professionals and teens, these removable aligners gradually move your teeth into proper position without the restrictions of traditional braces, giving you confidence throughout treatment.

Smile Makeovers

Combine multiple cosmetic treatments for a complete smile transformation tailored to your unique needs and goals. Our comprehensive approach addresses color, shape, size, and alignment issues to create your perfect smile through personalized treatment planning.

Gum Contouring

Reshape your gum line to create better symmetry and proportion in your smile. This gentle procedure can correct a "gummy" smile or uneven gum line, revealing more of your beautiful teeth and creating a more balanced, attractive appearance.
