Emergency Dental Services - Urgent Care

Professional Dental services tailored to your needs

At Connecticut Ave Dental Studio, we understand that dental emergencies can be frightening and painful. Our compassionate team provides prompt, professional emergency dental services to Cleveland Park residents and the surrounding DC area. When urgent dental issues arise, we're here to restore your comfort and oral health quickly.

Same-Day Emergency Appointments

We reserve time slots daily for dental emergencies, ensuring you receive care when you need it most. Whether you're experiencing severe tooth pain, a knocked-out tooth, or dental trauma, our team will see you promptly and provide immediate relief with gentle, effective treatment.

Severe Toothache Relief

Persistent tooth pain can disrupt your entire day and prevent restful sleep. Our emergency team quickly diagnoses the source of your discomfort and provides targeted pain relief. We address underlying issues like infections or damaged nerves while ensuring your immediate comfort throughout the treatment.

Broken or Chipped Tooth Repair

Accidents happen, and when they affect your smile, we're ready to help restore it. Our emergency repair services address fractured, chipped, or broken teeth using advanced techniques and materials. We work quickly to protect the damaged tooth structure and restore both function and appearance.

Knocked-Out Tooth Re-implantation

Time is critical when a tooth has been completely knocked out. Our emergency protocol maximizes the chances of successful re-implantation when you act quickly. We provide clear instructions over the phone and immediate in-office treatment to save your natural tooth whenever possible.

Dental Abscess and Infection Treatment

Dental infections require immediate attention to prevent serious complications. Our emergency care includes drainage procedures, antibiotic therapy, and pain management to address abscesses safely. We prioritize eliminating the infection while keeping you comfortable throughout the healing process.

Lost Filling or Crown Replacement

When fillings fall out or crowns become loose, your tooth is vulnerable to further damage and sensitivity. Our emergency services include temporary and permanent solutions to protect exposed tooth structure. We'll quickly restore your tooth's integrity and eliminate any discomfort you're experiencing.
