FAQ

How long does professional teeth whitening take?

Professional teeth whitening treatments typically take one to two hours, depending on the specific procedure and desired results.


Is professional teeth whitening safe?

Yes, professional teeth whitening performed by dental professionals is safe and effective when proper protocols are followed.


How long do whitening results last?

Results can last six months to two years, depending on your diet, lifestyle habits, and oral hygiene routine.

Will teeth whitening cause sensitivity?

Some patients may experience temporary sensitivity, which typically subsides within a few days after treatment.


Who is a good candidate for teeth whitening?

Most patients with healthy teeth and gums are good candidates. Contact us today to determine if professional whitening is right for you.
