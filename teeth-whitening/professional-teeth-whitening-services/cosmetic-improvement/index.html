Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our professional teeth whitening services.

How long does professional teeth whitening take?

Professional teeth whitening appointments typically take one to two hours, depending on your specific needs and desired results.

Is teeth whitening safe?

Yes, professional teeth whitening performed at Connecticut Ave Dental Studio uses safe, clinically-proven methods that protect your tooth enamel while effectively removing stains.

How long do whitening results last?

Results can last six months to two years, depending on your lifestyle habits like coffee consumption, smoking, and oral hygiene routine.

Some patients experience temporary sensitivity, but our professional approach minimizes discomfort compared to over-the-counter products.
