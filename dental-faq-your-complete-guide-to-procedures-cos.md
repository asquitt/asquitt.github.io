# Dental FAQ: Your Complete Guide to Procedures, Costs & Care Get clear answers about dental treatments, costs, and care. Make confident decisions about your oral health with expert guidance from our experienced team. ## How Much Do Common Dental Procedures Cost? **Routine cleanings**: $75-$200 **Fillings**: $150-$450 per tooth **Root canals**: $800-$1,500 **Dental crowns**: $1,000-$3,000 **Teeth whitening**: $300-$800 **Dental implants**: $3,000-$5,000 per tooth Most insurance plans cover preventive care at 100% and major procedures at 50-80%. ## What Happens During a Root Canal Procedure? Root canal therapy removes infected pulp from inside your tooth. The procedure takes 1-2 hours under local anesthesia. We clean the root canals, seal them, and place a temporary filling. Most patients return to normal activities the next day. ## How Long Do Dental Crowns Last? Quality dental crowns typically last 10-15 years with proper care. Porcelain crowns offer the most natural appearance, while metal crowns provide maximum durability for back teeth. ## What Should I Expect During My First Visit? Your initial appointment includes a comprehensive examination, digital X-rays, and personalized treatment planning. We'll review your dental history and address any concerns about upcoming treatments. ## How Often Should I Schedule Dental Cleanings? Most patients need cleanings every six months. Those with gum disease or high cavity risk may require visits every 3-4 months for optimal oral health. ## Are Dental X-Rays Safe? Yes! Modern digital X-rays use 90% less radiation than traditional film. Our advanced technology ensures your safety while providing clear diagnostic images for accurate treatment planning. ## What Payment Options Do You Accept?
