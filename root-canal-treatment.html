Root Canal Procedure: Complete Guide to Treatment & Recovery

The root canal procedure is a common dental treatment that saves infected or damaged teeth. At Connecticut Ave Dental Studio, we provide comprehensive care to help you understand this important treatment and feel confident throughout your root canal procedure.

Understanding Your Root Canal Procedure

A root canal procedure removes infected tissue from inside your tooth. This treatment preserves your natural tooth while eliminating pain and preventing further damage.

The process involves:

  • Removing infected pulp and bacteria
  • Cleaning and disinfecting the tooth's interior
  • Sealing the tooth to prevent reinfection
  • Restoring the tooth with a crown or filling

What to Expect During Treatment

Your root canal procedure typically requires one to three visits. Our experienced team uses modern equipment to ensure your comfort throughout the process.

Most patients experience:

  • Minimal discomfort during treatment
  • Quick recovery time
  • Long-lasting results
  • Preserved natural tooth function

Recovery and Aftercare

After your root canal procedure, proper care ensures optimal healing. Follow our team's aftercare instructions for the best results.

Recovery tips include:

  • Taking prescribed medications as directed
  • Avoiding hard foods initially
  • Maintaining good oral hygiene
  • Attending follow-up appointments
