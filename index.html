Our Story

Founded in 1998 by Chef Wei Zhang, Beijing Restaurant has been serving authentic Northern Chinese cuisine to the Washington DC community for over 25 years.

Chef Zhang trained at the prestigious Beijing Hotel before bringing his culinary expertise to the United States. Every dish reflects the rich traditions of Beijing's imperial cuisine, prepared with locally sourced ingredients whenever possible.

We take pride in our hand-pulled noodles, made fresh daily, and our Peking Duck, which requires a 48-hour preparation process for the perfect crispy skin.
