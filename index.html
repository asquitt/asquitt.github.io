Frequently Asked Questions

What insurance plans do you accept?
We accept most major dental insurance plans including Delta Dental, Cigna, Aetna, MetLife, and United Healthcare. We also offer flexible payment plans for uninsured patients.
How often should I visit the dentist?
We recommend visiting every six months for routine checkups and cleanings. However, patients with specific dental conditions may need more frequent visits.
Do you offer emergency dental services?
Yes! We offer same-day emergency appointments for dental emergencies including severe toothaches, broken teeth, lost fillings, and dental trauma.
What is the cost of teeth whitening?
Our professional teeth whitening treatments start at $299. We offer both in-office treatments and take-home kits for your convenience.

Dental Health Blog

The Importance of Regular Dental Checkups

Published: January 15, 2023 | By: Dr. Sarah Johnson

Regular dental checkups are essential for maintaining optimal oral health. During a routine visit, your dentist can identify potential problems before they become serious issues. Early detection of cavities, gum disease, and oral cancer can save you time, money, and discomfort in the long run.

We recommend visiting your dentist at least twice a year for professional cleanings and examinations. These visits help remove plaque and tartar buildup that regular brushing and flossing can't address.

5 Tips for Whiter Teeth Naturally

Published: March 22, 2022 | By: Dr. Michael Chen

Want a brighter smile without professional whitening? Here are some natural ways to keep your teeth looking their best:

  • Brush with baking soda once a week
  • Eat crunchy fruits and vegetables
  • Rinse with hydrogen peroxide diluted with water
  • Limit coffee and red wine consumption
  • Use a straw for dark-colored beverages
