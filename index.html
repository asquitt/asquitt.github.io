Frequently Asked Questions

Which insurance plans do you accept in 2025?
We accept all major dental insurance including Delta Dental, Cigna, Aetna, MetLife, United Healthcare, and Guardian. We also offer flexible financing options.
How often should I schedule dental visits?
Most patients benefit from visits every six months. Those with gum disease or high cavity risk may need quarterly appointments.
Do you provide emergency dental care?
Yes! We offer same-day emergency care for severe pain, broken teeth, lost fillings, and dental injuries. Call our emergency line anytime.
What does professional teeth whitening cost?
Our advanced whitening treatments start at $399. We offer both in-office laser whitening and custom take-home systems.
Do you offer payment plans?
Yes, we partner with CareCredit and offer in-house financing options with 0% interest for qualified patients.
