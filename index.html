Why Choose Connecticut Ave Dental Studio?

Comfort-Focused Care

Your comfort is our priority. We offer sedation dentistry, noise-canceling headphones, and a gentle approach to ensure a relaxing experience.

Advanced Technology

State-of-the-art digital imaging, CEREC same-day crowns, and laser dentistry for precise, efficient, and comfortable treatments.

Convenient DC Location

Perfectly located in the 20008 area, easily accessible from Cleveland Park, Woodley Park, Dupont Circle, and surrounding neighborhoods.

Comprehensive Dental Services

From routine cleanings to complete smile makeovers, we provide exceptional care for patients of all ages.

Preventive & General Dentistry

Regular cleanings, comprehensive exams, digital X-rays, and fluoride treatments to maintain optimal oral health.

Cosmetic Smile Enhancements

Professional teeth whitening, porcelain veneers, and cosmetic bonding to achieve your perfect "Capitol smile."

Invisalign & Clear Aligners

Discreet orthodontic treatment with clear aligners to straighten your teeth without traditional metal braces.

Emergency Dental Care

Same-day emergency appointments available for dental pain, broken teeth, and other urgent oral health concerns.

Restorative Dentistry

Crowns, bridges, fillings, and implant restoration to repair and replace damaged or missing teeth.

Periodontal Care

Comprehensive gum disease treatment and maintenance to preserve your oral health and prevent tooth loss.
