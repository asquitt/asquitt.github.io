Why Choose Sunrise Dental Care?

At Sunrise Dental Care, we believe everyone deserves a healthy, beautiful smile. Our experienced team of dental professionals is committed to providing the highest quality care in a comfortable, welcoming environment. Located in the heart of Austin, Texas, we have been serving families in the greater Austin area for over 15 years.

Our state-of-the-art facility features the latest dental technology, including digital X-rays, intraoral cameras, and laser dentistry equipment. We stay at the forefront of dental innovation to ensure our patients receive the most effective and comfortable treatments available. Whether you need a routine cleaning, cosmetic enhancement, or complex restorative procedure, our team has the expertise to help.

General Dentistry

Comprehensive exams, cleanings, fillings, and preventive care to maintain your oral health. We use gentle techniques and modern equipment to make every visit comfortable.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Teeth whitening, veneers, bonding, and smile makeovers to give you the confident smile you deserve. Our cosmetic treatments are tailored to your unique goals.

Emergency Care

Same-day appointments available for dental emergencies. Whether it is a toothache, broken tooth, or knocked-out tooth, we are here when you need us most.

Our Commitment to You

We understand that visiting the dentist can be stressful for some patients. That is why we prioritize creating a calm, reassuring atmosphere from the moment you walk through our doors. Our friendly staff will greet you with a smile, and our dental team will take the time to explain every procedure and answer all of your questions.

We accept most major dental insurance plans and offer flexible financing options to make quality dental care accessible to everyone. New patients are always welcome, and we offer special introductory rates for first-time visitors. Contact us today to schedule your appointment and experience the Sunrise Dental difference.

Serving the Austin Community

Conveniently located at 4521 Medical Parkway in Austin, TX, we serve patients from across the greater Austin area including Round Rock, Cedar Park, Pflugerville, Georgetown, and Lakeway. Our office is easily accessible with ample free parking available for all patients.

Office hours are Monday through Friday from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM, with Saturday appointments available by request. We strive to accommodate your busy schedule and minimize wait times so you can get back to your day quickly.
