Our Legacy

Founded in 1998 by Master Chef Wei Zhang, Beijing Restaurant has been DC's premier destination for authentic Northern Chinese cuisine for nearly three decades.

Chef Zhang trained at Beijing's prestigious Imperial Hotel before bringing his culinary mastery to America. Every dish honors Beijing's imperial culinary traditions using locally sourced ingredients.

We specialize in hand-pulled noodles made fresh daily, and our famous Peking Duck requires a traditional 48-hour preparation process for perfect crispy skin.
