Preventing Dental Emergencies: Tips from Your Washington DC Dentist

While some emergencies are unavoidable, many can be prevented with proper care and precautions. Maintaining good oral hygiene and regular check-ups are key.

Tips for preventing dental emergencies:

  • Wear a mouthguard during sports activities.
  • Avoid chewing on hard foods like ice or popcorn kernels.
  • Do not use your teeth to open packages.
  • Maintain a consistent oral hygiene routine.
  • Schedule regular dental check-ups and cleanings.

Regular visits to Connecticut Ave Dental Studio can help identify potential issues before they escalate into emergencies. We are committed to keeping your smile healthy and strong in 2026.

Can a knocked-out tooth be saved?

Yes, often. If a tooth is knocked out, hold it by the crown, rinse it gently (do not scrub), and try to reinsert it into the socket. If not possible, keep it moist in milk or saliva and get to our office within 30 minutes. Timely action is critical for successful reattachment.
