What Is Invisalign Clear Aligner Therapy?

Invisalign uses nearly invisible, removable aligners to straighten your teeth. These custom-made clear aligners gradually shift your teeth into proper position over time.

The process is simple:

  • Custom aligners designed for your unique smile
  • Wear aligners 20-22 hours daily
  • Switch to new aligners every 1-2 weeks
  • Regular check-ups to monitor progress

Most patients complete treatment within 12-18 months, though complex cases may take longer. We offer flexible payment options to make your smile transformation affordable.

Key Benefits of Invisalign Treatment

Our patients choose Invisalign for its convenience and discretion. The clear aligners are nearly invisible, so you can smile confidently during treatment. Remove them for eating, drinking, and cleaning your teeth.

Additional benefits include:

  • More comfortable than metal braces
  • Easier oral hygiene routine
  • No food restrictions
  • Fewer office visits required
  • Predictable treatment timeline

Are You a Candidate for Invisalign?

Invisalign effectively treats crowded teeth, gaps, overbites, underbites, and crossbites. During your consultation, our team will evaluate your specific needs and determine if Invisalign is right for you.
