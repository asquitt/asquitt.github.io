Our Story

Connecticut Avenue Dental was born from a simple belief: every person deserves to feel confident about their smile. Founded in the heart of Cleveland Park, our practice emerged from a desire to bring personalized, compassionate dental care to Washington DC families who value both excellence and genuine human connection.

Our mission centers on creating a warm, welcoming environment where patients feel heard, understood, and cared for as individuals—not just cases. We believe that quality dental care should be accessible, comfortable, and tailored to each person's unique needs and concerns. From routine cleanings to complex restorative work, we approach every treatment with the same dedication to excellence and patient comfort that has made us a trusted part of the Cleveland Park community.

Our Values

Compassionate Care: We understand that dental visits can feel overwhelming. Our team takes time to listen to your concerns, explain procedures clearly, and ensure you feel comfortable throughout your visit.

Clinical Excellence: We stay current with the latest dental techniques and technologies, combining proven methods with innovative approaches to deliver the highest standard of care.

Family-Centered Approach: We treat patients of all ages with the same gentle attention we'd want for our own families, building lasting relationships that span generations.

Transparency: We believe in honest communication about treatment options and costs, empowering you to make informed decisions about your oral health.

Why Choose Us

Comprehensive Care Under One Roof: From preventive cleanings to emergency care and cosmetic enhancements, we provide complete dental services, eliminating the need for multiple referrals and ensuring continuity of care.

Convenient Cleveland Park Location: Our Connecticut Avenue location offers easy access for DC residents, with flexible scheduling including Saturday hours to accommodate busy family schedules.

Personalized Treatment Plans: We recognize that no two smiles are alike. Our team develops customized treatment plans that align with your goals, timeline, and budget, ensuring you receive care that truly fits your life.
