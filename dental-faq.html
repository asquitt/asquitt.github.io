Dental Emergency Response

Immediate care for urgent situations:

  • Severe tooth pain: Urgent consultation
  • Knocked-out tooth: Preserve in milk, seek treatment within 1 hour
  • Broken tooth: Gentle rinse, prevent additional damage
  • Persistent bleeding: Apply gentle pressure
  • 24/7 emergency contact

Optimal Oral Hygiene Strategies

Expert recommendations for dental health:

  • Brush twice daily with fluoride toothpaste
  • Use electric toothbrush
  • Floss daily with proper technique
  • Rinse with antiseptic mouthwash
  • Limit sugary and acidic foods
  • Stay hydrated
  • Replace toothbrush every 3-4 months
