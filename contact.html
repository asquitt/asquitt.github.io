Contact Us

We'd love to hear from you

We're here to help you achieve your healthiest, most confident smile. Whether you have questions about our services, need to schedule an appointment, or require emergency dental care, our compassionate team at Connecticut Ave Dental Studio is ready to assist you. Don't hesitate to reach out – we understand that dental care decisions are important, and we're committed to making your experience as comfortable and stress-free as possible.

What to Expect

When you contact us, you'll speak with our friendly front office team who will listen carefully to your needs and concerns. We typically respond to calls during business hours and aim to return voicemails within 24 hours. For urgent dental matters, we offer emergency care options. Our team will help schedule your appointment at a convenient time and answer any questions about insurance, treatment options, or what to expect during your visit.
