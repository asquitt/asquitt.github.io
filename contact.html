New Patient Information

If you are a new patient, we recommend arriving 15 minutes early to complete the necessary paperwork. You can also download our new patient forms in advance to save time. Please bring your dental insurance card and a valid photo ID to your first appointment.

We accept most major dental insurance plans including Delta Dental, Cigna, Aetna, MetLife, Guardian, and United Healthcare. We also accept CareCredit financing for patients who prefer to spread the cost of treatment over time. Our billing coordinator can help you understand your coverage and maximize your benefits.
