# About Connecticut Ave Dental Studio

Welcome to Connecticut Ave Dental Studio, where world-class dental care meets neighborhood warmth in the heart of Washington DC. We've created a space that feels less like a clinic and more like a lounge, making your dental experience comfortable and stress-free.

## Meet Our Expert Team

Dr. Sarah Mitchell, DDS, leads our practice with over 15 years of experience serving the Washington DC community. Alongside Dr. James Chen, DMD, our team combines advanced training with a genuine passion for creating beautiful, healthy smiles. Both doctors are members of the American Dental Association and stay current with the latest dental innovations.

## Our Patient-First Philosophy

We believe dentistry should be personal and welcoming. Every patient receives customized treatment plans tailored to their unique needs and goals. Our team takes time to listen, explain procedures clearly, and ensure you feel confident about your care.

## Complete Dental Care Services

Our Connecticut Avenue studio offers comprehensive services:

• Preventive care and cleanings
• Cosmetic dentistry and smile makeovers
• Restorative treatments and implants
• Emergency dental services
• Digital imaging and laser dentistry

As proud members of the Washington DC community for over a decade, we understand the unique needs of busy professionals, families, and retirees in our neighborhood. Our modern studio combines cutting-edge technology with flexible scheduling to fit your lifestyle.
