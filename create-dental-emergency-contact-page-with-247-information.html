Common Washington DC Dental Emergencies

Recognize these urgent situations requiring immediate care:

  • Severe tooth pain or throbbing
  • Knocked-out or loose teeth
  • Broken or cracked teeth
  • Lost fillings or crowns
  • Swollen gums or facial swelling
  • Uncontrolled bleeding
  • Dental abscesses or infections

Emergency First Aid Instructions

Knocked-out teeth: Handle by crown only. Rinse gently. Reinsert immediately or store in milk. Severe pain: Rinse with warm salt water. Apply cold compress to cheek. Take over-the-counter pain medication as directed. Broken teeth: Save all pieces. Rinse with warm water. Apply clean gauze to bleeding areas for 10 minutes. Lost fillings: Clean area gently. Avoid chewing on affected side.
