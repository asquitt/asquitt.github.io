# Dental Insurance FAQ: Coverage, Payments & Benefits Guide

Navigating dental insurance can transform your patient care experience. Our comprehensive FAQ addresses your most pressing questions about coverage and payment options.

## What Does Dental Insurance Typically Cover?

Most plans cover:
- Preventive care: 100% coverage for cleanings and exams
- Basic treatments: 70-80% coverage for fillings
- Major procedures: 50% coverage for crowns and root canals

## How Do Dental Insurance Deductibles Work?

Your deductible is the amount you pay before insurance kicks in. Most dental plans have annual deductibles ranging from $50-$100 per person.

## What Payment Options Are Available?

We offer flexible solutions for your dental care:
- Insurance direct billing
- Monthly payment plans
- CareCredit financing
- HSA/FSA accepted

## When Should I Use My Annual Benefits?

Don't lose your benefits! Most plans reset annually. Schedule major treatments early in the year to maximize your coverage.

## Are Cosmetic Procedures Covered?

Cosmetic treatments like teeth whitening aren't typically covered. However, some procedures serve both functional and aesthetic purposes, making them eligible for partial coverage.

## How Can I Maximize My Insurance Benefits?

Plan strategically:
- Use preventive benefits fully
- Coordinate treatment timing
- Understand your plan's limitations
