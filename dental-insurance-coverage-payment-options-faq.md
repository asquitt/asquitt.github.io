# Dental Insurance Coverage & Payment Options FAQ

Navigating dental insurance can feel overwhelming. We've compiled the most common questions to help you understand your coverage and discover affordable care options.

## Does My Insurance Cover Preventive Care?

Most dental insurance plans cover 100% of preventive treatments like cleanings and exams. These visits help maintain your smile and catch issues early.

## What About Major Dental Procedures?

Insurance typically covers:
- Basic procedures: 70-80% (fillings, extractions)
- Major treatment: 50% (crowns, bridges)
- Advanced procedures may have waiting periods
