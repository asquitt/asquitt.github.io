Frequently Asked Questions About Dental Services in Capitol Hill

What dental services do you offer?

Connecticut Ave Dental Studio provides comprehensive dental care including routine cleanings, fillings, crowns, root canals, and cosmetic treatments. Our team focuses on preventive care to maintain your oral health.


How often should I schedule dental checkups?

Most patients benefit from dental visits every six months for cleanings and examinations. However, your specific needs may require more frequent appointments based on your oral health condition.


Do you accept dental insurance?

We work with most major dental insurance plans. Contact us to verify your coverage and discuss payment options that fit your budget.

What should I expect during my first visit?

Your initial appointment includes a comprehensive examination, digital X-rays if needed, and a thorough cleaning. We'll discuss your dental history and create a personalized treatment plan.


How do I prepare for my appointment?

Bring your insurance information and a list of current medications. Arrive a few minutes early to complete any necessary paperwork.
