Emergency Dental Care Washington DC | Urgent Dental Treatment

A dental emergency can strike without warning—severe pain, a knocked-out tooth, or sudden injury that demands immediate attention. When every minute counts, Connecticut Ave Dental Studio provides urgent Emergency Care to relieve your pain and protect your smile.

When You Need Urgent Dental Treatment

Dental emergencies require prompt care to prevent permanent damage. Our Emergency Care team handles critical situations that can't wait for regular appointments.

Common dental emergencies include:

  • Knocked-out or broken teeth
  • Severe tooth pain or swelling
  • Lost fillings or crowns
  • Cuts to lips, gums, or tongue
  • Jaw injuries or fractures

Time matters with dental trauma. Quick treatment improves your chances of saving damaged teeth and preventing complications.

Our Emergency Care Approach

We understand dental emergencies cause stress and discomfort. Our team provides compassionate Emergency Care using modern equipment and advanced techniques.

Our emergency services include:

  • Pain relief and infection control
  • Tooth stabilization and repair
  • Temporary restorations
  • Soft tissue injury treatment
  • Emergency extractions when necessary

Our Emergency Care connects seamlessly with our General Dentistry and Comprehensive Care services, ensuring both immediate relief and long-term treatment planning.

What to Expect During Your Emergency Visit

When you arrive, we prioritize your comfort and pain relief. We begin with a thorough examination and use digital imaging to understand the damage and plan treatment.

Our emergency protocols ensure efficient care while maintaining the highest safety standards. We explain each step and provide clear aftercare instructions for your recovery.
