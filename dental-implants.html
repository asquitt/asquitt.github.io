About ConnAve Dental

Our Story & Mission

ConnAve Dental was founded with a clear vision: every person deserves exceptional dental care in a welcoming, comfortable environment. Our practice serves the Cleveland Park community and surrounding Washington DC neighborhoods with compassionate dentistry that treats patients like family members, not just appointments.

Since 2026, we've combined cutting-edge dental techniques with genuine human connection. We understand dental visits can feel overwhelming, which is why we've built our practice around creating positive experiences that leave patients confident about their oral health. Our mission goes beyond treating teeth – we support our community's overall well-being, one smile at a time.

Our Core Values

Patient-Centered Care - Every patient deserves to feel heard, respected, and comfortable throughout their dental journey, regardless of their dental history or concerns.

Clinical Excellence - Our commitment to advanced training and modern technology ensures every patient receives the highest quality dental care available in Washington DC.

Community Partnership - As proud Cleveland Park neighbors, we're dedicated to building lasting relationships with local families and supporting our community's health needs.

Clear Communication - We explain treatments thoroughly, discuss all options, and ensure patients feel empowered to make informed decisions about their dental health.

Why Patients Choose ConnAve Dental

Complete Dental Care - From preventive cleanings to advanced implants and emergency care, our comprehensive services mean you receive all dental treatments conveniently in one trusted location.

Family-First Philosophy - We welcome patients of all ages and understand busy Washington DC families' unique needs. Our flexible scheduling and gentle approach make dental care accessible and stress-free for everyone.

Emergency Dental Support - Dental emergencies happen at inconvenient times. We prioritize urgent care and ensure our Cleveland Park neighbors receive prompt, professional treatment when unexpected dental issues arise.
